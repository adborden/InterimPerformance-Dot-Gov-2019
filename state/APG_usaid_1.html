United States Agency for International Development  

Food Security and Resilience

Goal Leaders: Beth Dunford, Assistant to the Administrator, Bureau for Food Security

Sean Jones, Senior Deputy Assistant to the Administrator, Bureau for Food Security

Goal Statement: Increase food security and resilience in Feed the Future countries. By September 30, 2019, Feed the Future will exhibit an average reduction in the prevalence of poverty and stunting of 20 percent, across target regions in Feed the Future’s focus countries, since the beginning of the initiative in FY 2010.

Implementation Action Plans will be available with the Q1 FY 2018 performance update.
